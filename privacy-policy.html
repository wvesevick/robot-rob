Robot Rob Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

App Name: Robot Rob

Game Mode: Vanishing Man

Owner/Publisher: Robot Rob

Short version: Robot Rob does not collect, store, share, or sell personal information. The app is designed for children and runs entirely on-device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from children or any users. The app does not require account creation and does not request names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, photos, contacts, or identifiers.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Robot Rob is intended for children in Pre-K through 3rd grade. In line with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

3. Data Usage and Storage

The app functions without server-side user tracking. Game progress is used only during gameplay on the device and is not transmitted to us.

4. Third-Party Services

Robot Rob does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or external trackers.

5. In-App Purchases and Payments

Robot Rob is a free app and does not include in-app purchases.

6. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

7. Security

Since no personal data is collected by us, there is no personal profile database maintained by Robot Rob.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the Effective Date above.
