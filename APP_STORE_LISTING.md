## Description
Robot Rob is a playful word-guessing game made for children in Pre-K through 3rd grade.

In Vanishing Man mode, each wrong guess makes part of Robot Rob vanish. Kids race the timer, solve words, and celebrate wins with cheerful sound effects.

What kids can do:
- Pick their grade: Pre-K, Kindergarten, 1st, 2nd, or 3rd
- Choose grade-based categories
- Play the special Robot Rob Mystery category for mixed word challenges
- Learn vowel and consonant patterns with color support
- Use visual cartoon clues for each word

Designed for children:
- Kid-friendly interface
- No account required
- No ads
- No in-app purchases
- No personal data collection

Robot Rob blends playful challenge and early literacy support without feeling like homework.
